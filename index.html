Objectives: Learning Model Architecture

Enable pathway students in the field of programming and computer science throughout South Africa to interact and realize their full potential by applying the BYU Idaho learning model: "Prepare, Teach, and Ponder.."

  1. PREPARE: This is a spiritual preparation for each student. It requires daily attention to prayer and scripture study, and a commitment to obedience (Learning Model, 2007). It also requires organizing required readings or assignments by reading and studying...

  2. TEACH ONE ANOTHER:

      Here the emphasis is on "TEACHING TO LEARN"

  3. PONDER AND PROVE:

      PONDER means taking time to reflect on what is being learned to help solidify and relate the new concepts, ideas, or perceptive being taught.

ABOUT
